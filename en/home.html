Peace, comfort, and a view that never gets old!

Our apartments are situated in a magical location in the heart of Šumava’s nature, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and peat bogs. Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat for a family vacation or a place to gather with friends, you'll feel right at home with us. Thoughtfully designed spaces ensure everyone has their privacy while still providing plenty of room for shared moments and unforgettable experiences.

Komfort a vybavení pro váš dokonalý pobyt

Apartmány Na Výsluní are located in the Šumava Protected Landscape Area (CHKO Šumava), right on the border of the Šumava National Park, just 11 km from Lipno Reservoir. They are part of the Panorama Chlum Resort project.

Situated on a sunny hillside in a peaceful location within the CHKO, these apartments offer a tasteful combination of modern accommodation and stunning nature.

They are the perfect choice for a tranquil family vacation. Guests can immerse themselves in the breathtaking views of the valley, the Šumava hills, and the nearby peat bogs.

Designed with attention to detail, the apartments provide a warm and homely atmosphere. High-quality furniture, tasteful decorations, and modern amenities create a harmonious space for relaxation and comfort.
